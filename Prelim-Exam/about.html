About the Philippine Red Cross

The Philippine Red Cross has been a beacon of hope during times of crisis. We are an independent, volunteer-based humanitarian organization providing essential services to those in need.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide humanitarian aid, emergency relief, and care to those in need, regardless of nationality, race, or religion. We are committed to alleviating human suffering, protecting life, and ensuring the dignity of individuals during emergencies.

Our Vision

We envision a future where the Philippine Red Cross is a symbol of hope, resilience, and peace. We strive for a future where every Filipino has access to essential services, particularly during disasters and emergencies.

Core Values

  • Humanity: Providing assistance regardless of background or beliefs.
  • Impartiality: Serving without discrimination based on race, religion, or politics.
  • Neutrality: Remaining neutral in all conflicts.
  • Independence: Remaining autonomous in our actions.
  • Voluntary Service: Offering services without personal gain.
  • Unity: Working together as one Red Cross family.
  • Universality: Promoting solidarity across nations.

History of the Philippine Red Cross

Founded in 1947, the Philippine Red Cross began as a response to the humanitarian needs of the country. Over the decades, it has evolved into one of the largest and most trusted humanitarian organizations, consistently responding to the challenges faced by our communities.

Leadership

Our leadership team consists of experienced individuals who are committed to the principles of humanitarian action. Together with our volunteers and staff, they work tirelessly to ensure that we meet the needs of vulnerable communities across the nation.

How We Operate

The Philippine Red Cross operates in partnership with the government, other non-governmental organizations, and international agencies. We adhere to the guiding principles of the International Red Cross and Red Crescent Movement to ensure effective and neutral humanitarian aid.

Get Involved

Join us in making a difference. Whether you volunteer, donate, or simply support our initiatives, every bit of help contributes to a stronger, safer, and more resilient community.
