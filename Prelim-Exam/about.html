About the Philippine Red Cross

Founded in 1947, the Philippine Red Cross has been a beacon of hope during times of crisis. We offer services ranging from blood donation to disaster relief.

Mission

Our mission is to provide humanitarian aid to those in need and build a stronger, more resilient community.

Vision

We envision a country where every Filipino lives in peace and safety, with access to quality healthcare and protection during crises.
